TN IMPACT 2026

About This Project

A hackathon project built for TN IMPACT 2026 — a smart platform that helps government departments and private companies manage their money better, all in one place.

What is this project?

Most organizations track money using different apps, spreadsheets, and tools that don't talk to each other. This creates confusion, budget leaks, and missed problems. Our platform brings all that financial data into one dashboard — making it easy to see, control, and manage spending.

Built for Government
Government departments can upload their spending data and instantly see how budgets are being used — department by department. No more manual audits or missing reports.
Built for Enterprises
Private companies can connect their tools via API and track vendor costs, project budgets, and team spending in real time — without switching between different apps.
Catches Overspending
The platform automatically flags unusual spending, budget overruns, and policy violations — before they become a big problem.
Easy to Understand
All data is shown through clean charts and dashboards. Even non-technical users can see exactly where money is going and make smarter decisions.

How It Works

Simple 4-step process — from data to decisions.

Upload Your Data
Upload a CSV or Excel file with spending data, or connect via API. The platform accepts data from any source.
Auto-Organize
The system automatically sorts expenses by department, project, and vendor — so you don't have to do it manually.
Detect Problems
The platform checks spending against budget rules and flags anything that looks wrong — overspending, unusual transactions, or policy violations.
View & Report
See live dashboards, download audit-ready reports, and share insights with your team — all from one simple interface.

About the Team

This project was built as part of TN IMPACT 2026 — a state-level hackathon organized by the Government of Tamil Nadu to solve real-world problems using technology. Our goal was to build a practical, working FinOps platform that can be used by both government institutions and private enterprises across Tamil Nadu and beyond.
